About Me

Some people say I am an interesting fellow. I actually find that interesting, since I consider myself a simple guy. Simple. at least, is how I think things should be. And I do not consider me or anyone particularly important. I am aware of our cosmic insignificance.

Although I have worked for a web portal during the .com bubble, I then moved on to finance and my web skills became rusted.. While I kept the habbit of looking at the source code of web pages, I grew to believe CSS and HTML 5 were rather difficult. Javascript seemed to be enhanced by how it can affect the page using CSS.

Aside from finance and web, and being a daddy for my two boys, I find time to do some cool stuff... So here are some of the things I do as a hobby:

  • Fly airplanes and helicopters. Yes, I'm an IFR-rated pilot. The sky is my therapist. Ferrying a Cirrus from Duluth, MN to Brazil was a very cool flight experience.
  • Build and fly multirotor drones, then make videos.
  • Ski. Lots of it.
  • Scuba Dive or just boat around.
  • Fix and build stuff in general.
  • Read. I like Ayn Rand, Richard Dawkins and Stephen Hawking in particular.
  • Cook, eat and drink well.

That's it for a short list, but since I've been around for quite some time now, there is more to it then this short page. Maybe over a beer I can tell you more about me and learn about you, too!
